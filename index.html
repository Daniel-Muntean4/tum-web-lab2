Our Story

In 1987, Nonna Maria left her small village near Naples with little more than a suitcase and a handwritten recipe book. What started as weekend dinners for friends in a tiny apartment kitchen grew into something the whole neighborhood looked forward to.

Today, Bella Notte continues that tradition. Every dish is prepared with the same recipes, the same care, and the same belief that a good meal brings people together. We don't rush. We don't cut corners. We cook the way Nonna taught us.
